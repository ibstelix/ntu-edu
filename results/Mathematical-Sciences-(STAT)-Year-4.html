This is the first course in optimization and operations research. MTH316 ORDINARY DIFFERENTIAL EQUATIONS 4.0 AU
Prerequisite: MAS212 OR
MTH211
Mutually exclusive with: MAS321, MH3110
Not available to Programme: MAEC(2004-2009), MAEC(2011-2050), MATH(2011-2050)
First order equations, exact equations, integrating factors, separable equations, linear homogeneous and non-homogeneous equations, variation of parameters, Principle of superposition. Use of ODE in simple modeling problems.
