CV4116 COASTAL ENGINEERING 3.0 AU
Prerequisite: CV2602 OR
EN3601 OR
CV2015
Mutually exclusive with: CV4751
Not available to Programme: CEE(2004-2010), CEE(2004-2011)
Wind-generated waves: wind wave generation, statistics and forecasting. Mechanics of wave motion: linear wave theory, wave kinematics, pressure, energy and power, wave group celerity, shoaling, refraction, diffraction and reflection of waves, wave breaking and runups. Coastal wave level fluctuations: tides, seiches, surges, wind setup and basin oscillations. Coastal zone processes: beach profiles, longshore transport and sediment budget. Coastal structures: fluid loading, breakwater design.
 
EN4102 MEMBRANE WATER RECLAMATION TECHNOLOGY 3.0 AU
Prerequisite: Year 3 standing
Mutually exclusive with: EN4556
Introduction to membrane technology. Membrane fundamentals including working principles, process control and design. Microfiltration (MF), ultrafiltration (UF), nanofiltration (NF) and reverse osmosis (RO). Prevention and control of membrane fouling. NEWater production. Recovery and reuse of secondary effluent. Membrane desalination. Membrane distillation. Membrane-bioreactor.
 
HW0310 PROFESSIONAL COMMUNICATION 2.0 AU

Mutually exclusive with: HW310
Not available to Programme: MS(MSB) 1, MS(MSB) 2

This course aims to equip students with the oral and written communication skills essential for functioning effectively in the workplace. Teaching and learning is conducted in the context of a globalised and constantly changing work environment with emphasis placed on flexibility and persuasiveness in communication. The focus is on written as well as oral professional communication.
