MS4621 POLYMER SCIENCE 3.0 MAT Prerequisite: MS2003 & MS2010 OR MS1014 & MS2013 Mutually exclusive with: MS4554 It covers polymer chemistry and physics. The polymer chemistry part covers polymerization and copolymerization methods that involve different mechanisms, and industrial polymerization processes. In polymer physics part, the concept of average molecular weights and polydispersity will be briefly revisited, and the principles and techniques used to measure molecular weights are introduced. Factors affecting polymer properties are being taught that include polymer chain structures, the importance of thermal transitions in polymers, relationship between polymer microstructures and mechanical properties. MS8205 MATERIALS FOR ENERGY CONVERSION 3.0 MAT Introduction to materials and fuels for conventional energy technology. In this part, the emphasis will be given on the basics of chemistry, physics and thermodynamics of energy conversion. The efficiency of existing technologies will be evaluated. Following topics will be included Conception and units in energy conversion. Fossil fuel energy sources. Efficiency and resources. Materials for energy transport. Energy storage Materials for renewable energy generation. In this part, the renewable resources will be presented and it will be shown that mostly material properties limit the efficiency and applications of alternative energy technologies. Materials issue in following subjects will be discussed: Thermoelectric materials. Solar radiation. Semiconductors for photovoltaics. Silicon solar cells. Large area solar cells. Compound semiconductor solar cells. Organic solar cells. Solar modules. Biomass and biogas. Material issues in fuel cells. Wind energy, hydroelectric power, geothermal power. Hydrogen as a fuel and energy storage material Materials for sustainable development. In this parts materials conservation, recycling and sustainable use will be discussed. Water as energy source and energy transmitter. Materials and energy conservation and recycling. Materials for sustainable life
