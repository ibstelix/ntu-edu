HW0210 TECHNICAL COMMUNICATION 2.0 AU
Mutually exclusive with: HW210
Not available to all Programme: (Admyr 2014-2050)-Non Direct Entry, (Admyr 2015-2050)-Direct Entry
This course aims to teach students principles of technical communication for their academic and professional needs. The course focuses on basic written and oral skills essential in presenting technical information effectively. A key feature of the course is the use of a project-based learning approach in learning technical communication.

Mutually exclusive with: MS3001

MS3015 MATERIALS ASPECTS IN DESIGN 3.0 AU
Prerequisite: MS1015 & MS2013(Corequisite) & MS3011(Corequisite) OR
MS2005(Corequisite) & MS2010(Corequisite) & MS3001(Corequisite) OR
FE1005 & MS2010(Corequisite) & MS3001(Corequisite)
Mutually exclusive with: MS3005
This course introduces theory of design in an intermediate to advanced level. 1. Principle of design process, motivation and translation. 2. Develop charts to rank materials according the objective. Use charts to obtain the global overview of engineering materials and develop strategy in the design of new materials. 3. A 10 week group design project on materials-related topics
