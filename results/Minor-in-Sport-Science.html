COURSE CODE TITLE AU PROGRAMME/(DEPT MAINTAIN*)
SS1004 INTRODUCTION TO GROWTH & MOTOR DEVELOPMENT 3.0 SSM(NIE) This course is designed for the understanding of physical growth and motor development, particularly during childhood and adolescence. The impact of age-related changes, individual and sex-differences, maturity-associated variation on physical growth and motor development will be covered. SS1005 HEALTH & WELLNESS I 3.0 SSM(NIE) Mutually exclusive with: ABA105 Health & Wellness Heath Education, Prevention & Promotion Substance Abuse/Smoking Cancer/STDs & AIDS Nutritional Concerns Weight Management Body Image Disorders Stress Management SS1006 EXERCISE & SPORT PHYSIOLOGY I 3.0 SSM(NIE) Mutually exclusive with: ABA106 A lecture/demonstration laboratory course is designed as a foundation course for the study of exercise physiology. Emphasis will be placed on; the energy systems that fuel muscular work, muscle fiber types, aerobic and anaerobic performance and training adaptations, blood lactate after exercise and maximal oxygen uptake. The importance of proper fluid management and aspects of thermoregulation during exercise in the local tropical environment will also be emphasised. SS2001 EXERCISE & SPORT PSYCHOLOGY 1 3.0 SSM(NIE) Mutually exclusive with: ABA201 This focus of this module is on the social and psychological aspects of sport and exercise. Topics covered include personality, motivation, stress and coping, aggression, moral development, team building and cohesion, competition, and leadership. Emphasis will be on the role of sport psychology in influencing participants' cognition, emotion, and behaviour in physical activity and sport settings. SS2007 SPORT INJURIES I 3.0 SSM(NIE) Mutually exclusive with: ABA207 This module is designed to provide students with fundamental knowledge and skills to prevent and manage sports injuries. The module covers background knowledge in dealing with sports injuries from first aid care to rehabilitation.
