CY1400 CN YANG SCHOLARS PROGRAMME UNDERGRADUATE RESEARCH EXPERIENCE 3.0 BS
Mutually exclusive with: CNYPR, PAP119, PH1199
This module provides undergraduates with the opportunity to carry out research during the semester with one or more faculty members. Student will be required to conduct research work under supervision. Enrollment will be limited to students who have gained entry to the scholar programme or by permission of the instructor with approval from Director of the CN Yang Scholars Programme. Objectives This module will train students the necessary skills required to do research. Learning Outcomes The students will acquire the necessary skills to carry out research. After taking this course, they will be well prepared to carry out more independent research. Module Assessment Students will be assessed by: Continual Assessment (report, lab book writing, data collection, presentation, lab techniques, etc) (100%)
 
CY1202 CALCULUS OF SEVERAL VARIABLES 4.0 SPS
Prerequisite: MATH1A OR
CY1201
Mutually exclusive with: MAS112, MAS113, MAS182, MAS211, MAS212, MATH1B, MH1101, MH1801, MH2100, MTH113, MTH211
Calculus of several variables: Parametric equations and polar coordinates. Vectors in R2 and R3. Vector functions. Partial derivatives, limits and continuity, chain rule, directional derivatives, gradients, Lagrange multipliers. Double integrals, area of a surface, triple integrals. Vector calculus, line integrals, Green's Theorem, surface integrals, Gauss's divergence theorem, Stokes's Theorem. Basics of Linear Algebra: Sets, operations on sets, properties of sets. Systems of linear equations, Gaussian elimination. Matrices, inverses, determinants. Vector spaces, subspaces, linear independence, basis, dimension, row and column spaces, rank.
